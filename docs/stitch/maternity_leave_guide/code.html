MOM/MSF Policy Framework

16-Week Coverage

The Government-Paid Maternity Leave (GPML) scheme covers all 16 weeks of leave. For self-employed individuals, the government pays for the entire duration, capped at $10,000 per 4-week period.

Weeks 1–8: Gov Funded
Weeks 9–16: Gov Funded

Notice Period

Self-employed persons should keep records of their intent to take leave at least 1 week prior.

Pro Tips for Entrepreneurs

Managing a business while on leave requires careful planning. Here is how to maximize your benefits while keeping your business afloat.

1

Income Evidence: Keep your Notice of Assessment (NOA) from IRAS ready. This is the primary document used to calculate your daily rate.

2

Hiring a Substitute: Expenses incurred to hire a temporary replacement while you are on leave are not reimbursable, but they help prove your "loss of trade income".

lightbulb

Claim Window

You must submit your claim through the Government-Paid Leave (GPL) Portal no later than 3 months after the last day of your leave.
